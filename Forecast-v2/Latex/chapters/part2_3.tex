\section{Describe a city you enjoyed visiting}
\begin{ieltsprompt}[IELTS Part 2 — Cue Card]
Describe a city you enjoyed visiting. \\
You should say: \\
\textbullet\ Where it is \\
\textbullet\ When you visited it \\
\textbullet\ How long you stayed there \\
\textbullet\ What you did there \\
\textbullet\ And explain why you enjoyed visiting it. \\
\end{ieltsprompt}

\vspace{1em}
\textit{Bài trả lời mẫu Part 2 sẽ được cập nhật tại đây (150-200 từ)...}
\vspace{2em}

\begin{ieltsprompt}[IELTS Part 3 — Discussion Questions]
\begin{enumerate}[itemsep=4pt]
    \item What kinds of facilities do big cities have?
    \item Do you think modern cities are suitable for young people or old people?
    \item Before you travel to a city, what factors would you consider?
    \item What are the disadvantages of living in a very famous city?
    \item Do you prefer to visit well-developed cities or cities with a long history?
    \item For those who live in cities, is it because they want to or have to?
\end{enumerate}
\end{ieltsprompt}
