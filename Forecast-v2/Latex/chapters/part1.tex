\section{Work or Studies}
\begin{ieltsprompt}[IELTS Part 1 — Work or Studies]
\begin{itemize}[itemsep=4pt]
    \item What work do you do?
    \item What subjects are you studying?
    \item Do you like your subject?
    \item Do you like your job?
    \item Why did you choose to study that subject?
    \item Why did you choose to do that type of work (or that job)?
    \item What requirements did you need to meet to get your current job?
    \item Do you think that your subject is popular in your country?
    \item Do you have any plans for your studies in the next five years?
    \item Do you have any plans for your work in the next five years?
    \item Do you prefer to study in the mornings or in the afternoons?
    \item How much time do you spend on your studies each week?
    \item Do you want to change your major?
    \item Do you want to change to another job?
    \item Are you looking forward to working?
    \item Do you miss being a student?
    \item What technology do you use when you study?
    \item What technology do you use at work?
    \item What changes would you like to see in your school?
    \item Who helps you the most? And how?
    \item What do you think is the most important at the moment?
    \item What are the benefits of being your age?
\end{itemize}
\end{ieltsprompt}
