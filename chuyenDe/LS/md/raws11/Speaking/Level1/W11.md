## PART 1

### Hometown

#### 1. Where is your hometown?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- My hometown is Hanoi.
- It is the capital city of Vietnam.
- I have lived there since I was born.

My hometown is Hanoi, which is the capital city of Vietnam. It is located in the north of the country. I have lived there since I was born with my family, and I really like living in such a central place.

#### 2. Is that a big city or a small place?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- It is a very big city with many people.
- There are many tall buildings and shops.
- It is often very crowded and noisy.

#### 3. Please describe your hometown a little

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- There are many beautiful lakes and parks.
- The streets are full of motorbikes every day.
- You can find many cheap and delicious street foods.

#### 4. What\'s your hometown famous for?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- It is famous for a traditional food called Pho.
- It is also known for the Old Quarter.
- Many tourists come to see the historic temples.
- Museum

#### 1. Are there many museums in your hometown?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Yes, there are many museums in my city.
- Most of them are in the center of the city.
- They show many old things from the past.

Yes, there are many museums in my city because it is a very big place. Most of them are located in the center of the city, so it is easy to go there. They show many old things from the past, like clothes and old tools.

#### 2. Do you often visit a museum?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- No, I do not go there very often.
- I am usually very busy with my study.
- I only go when I have a school trip.

#### 3. When was the last time you visited a museum?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- The last time was two months ago.
- I went to the History Museum with my friends.
- We saw many interesting pictures there.

#### 4. Do you think museums are important?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Yes, I think they are very important.
- They help us learn about our history.
- Children can learn many things there.

## PART 2

::: prompt
**Describe a wild animal that you want to learn more about**
- What it is
- When/where you saw it
- Why you want to learn more about it
- And explain what you want to learn more about it
:::

I would like to talk about a wild animal that I find very interesting, which is the snow leopard. I first saw this animal in a nature documentary on TV about a month ago. Before that, I only knew about normal lions and tigers, but the snow leopard looked very different and beautiful. It has thick, white and grey fur with black spots, which helps it hide in the snow.

I want to learn more about this animal because it is very rare and lives in high mountains where it is very cold. I think it is amazing how they can survive in such a difficult place. I want to know more about their daily life, for example, how they find food and how they protect their babies in the winter. Also, I heard that they are \"endangered,\" which means there are not many of them left in the world. I want to understand what people are doing to save them. Learning about them makes me feel more connected to nature and realize that we need to protect our planet\'s beautiful animals.

## PART 3

#### 1. Is it more important to protect wild animals or the environment?

I think both are very important, but protecting the environment is more necessary. This is because the environment is the home of all wild animals. If we protect the forests and the oceans, then the animals will have a safe place to live and find food. Without a good environment, animals cannot survive even if we try to help them. Therefore, keeping the earth clean and green is the most important thing for everyone to do.

#### 2. Should people educate children to protect wild animals?

Yes, I believe we should teach children about this from a young age. Children are the future, so if they learn to love and respect animals now, they will protect them when they grow up. Schools can show them documentaries or take them to the zoo to see real animals. This helps them understand that animals are an important part of our world. Educating kids is the best way to make sure animals stay safe forever.

#### 3. Why do some people attach more importance to protecting rare animals?

I think people care more about rare animals because they are in danger of disappearing forever. If a rare animal like a panda dies out, we can never see it again. Many people feel sad about this loss of nature. Also, rare animals often look very special or beautiful, so they get more attention on the news and social media. People want to save these unique creatures before it is too late for the whole species.
