## Phần 1: Section 1

### 1. Hướng dẫn cách làm bài: Dạng bài Form Completion

**Bước 1: Phân tích đề bài và giới hạn từ**

- Trong toàn bộ 10 câu, đề bài yêu cầu ***ONE WORD AND/OR A NUMBER.*** Mỗi chỗ trống được điền **tối đa 1 từ** và **1 số**.

**Bước 2: Đọc phần câu hỏi và dự đoán câu trả lời**

- **Câu 1:** *always accompanied* → trẻ em luôn đi kèm với ai? → **danh từ chỉ người**.
- **Câu 2:** *Cost* → giá khóa học 2 ngày → **số tiền**.
- **Câu 3:** *receive, last day* + mạo từ *a* → nhận được thứ gì đó vào ngày cuối → **danh từ đếm được số ít**.
- **Câu 4:** *must wear* → trang phục bắt buộc → **danh từ chỉ phụ kiện/trang phục**.
- **Câu 5:** *recommended, extra trousers* → mô tả loại quần → **tính từ**.
- **Câu 6:** *not allowed* → trang phục bị cấm → **danh từ chỉ trang phục**.
- **Câu 7:** *buy, Marina Stores* + mạo từ *a* → mua được gì ở đó → **danh từ đếm được số ít**.
- **Câu 8:** *free car park, next to* → bãi đỗ xe miễn phí cạnh nơi nào → **danh từ chỉ địa điểm**.
- **Câu 9:** *include, name, Jacob's* → tên của ai/cái gì liên quan đến Jacob → **danh từ**.
- **Câu 10:** *optional form, permission* → đơn cho phép làm gì → **danh từ**.

### 2. Phân tích chi tiết từng câu hỏi:

### Question 1

**Phân tích:**
- *"the two-day course just covers basic techniques. And on this course, **the kids always sail with the instructor they've been allocated.**"*
- *always sail with the instructor* → luôn luôn được đi kèm bởi người hướng dẫn.

**Đáp án: instructor**

### Question 2

**Phân tích:**
- *"the cost is **£65**."* → Người phụ nữ nhầm với £125 của khóa 5 ngày, nhưng người đàn ông xác nhận đó là giá khóa 5 ngày.

**Đáp án: 65**

### Question 3

**Phân tích:**
- *"do you give them a certificate if they complete the course?"* → người đàn ông xác nhận: *"Absolutely. We give them out on the **final day**."*
- Từ khóa *final day* = *last day*, vật nhận được là **certificate**.

**Đáp án: certificate**

### Question 4

**Phân tích:**
- *"the only rule is that **they've got to have shoes** of some kind. They mustn't have bare feet."*
- *must have shoes* = *must wear shoes*.

**Đáp án: shoes**

### Question 5

**Phân tích:**
- *"we suggest the kids pack an extra pair of trousers. You know, **waterproof** ones."*
- *don't have to wear them unless it starts to rain* → khuyến nghị (*recommended*), không bắt buộc.

**Đáp án: waterproof**

### Question 6

**Phân tích:**
- *"the children **mustn't wear jeans** because they get extremely heavy when they're wet."*
- *mustn't* = *not allowed*.

**Đáp án: jeans**

### Question 7

**Phân tích:**
- *"we do have a shop called the Marina Stores where he can **get a snack**."*
- *get* = *buy*, *snack* là thứ có thể mua được.

**Đáp án: snack**

### Question 8

**Phân tích:**
- *"there's no charge for the **car park** just **by the bridge**."*
- *no charge* = *free*, *by* = *next to*.

**Đáp án: bridge**

### Question 9

**Phân tích:**
- Người phụ nữ hỏi: *"Will you need to know who **his doctor** is?"* → Người đàn ông xác nhận: *"Yes."*
- Cần điền tên bác sĩ (*name of Jacob's doctor*).

**Đáp án: doctor**

### Question 10

**Phân tích:**
- *"there's a separate **form** on the website, which is **optional**, that asks whether you're willing for **photographs to be taken** during the course."*
- *optional* = *optional form*, *photographs to be taken* = *permission to take photographs*.

**Đáp án: photographs**

## Phần 2: Section 2

> Section 2 là một bài **độc thoại** (monologue) về một chủ đề thực tế. Dạng bài ở đây bao gồm **Multiple Choice** (câu 11--15) và **Matching** (câu 16--20).

### 1. Hướng dẫn chiến lược làm bài

**Đối với Multiple Choice (Câu 11--15): Choose A, B or C**

- **Đọc kỹ câu hỏi:** Gạch chân từ khóa quan trọng. Các đáp án sai có thể được nhắc đến trong bài nhưng bị phủ định hoặc không phải lý do/đặc điểm chính.
- **Cảnh giác với bẫy:** Nếu nghe thấy từ trùng khớp với đáp án, đừng vội chọn — hãy đợi nghe trọn câu.

**Đối với Matching (Câu 16--20): Choose FIVE from A--G**

- Thông tin trong bài nghe theo thứ tự từng địa điểm (16 → 17 → 18 → ...).
- Mỗi đáp án chỉ được dùng **một lần**.
- Đáp án sai thường được nhắc đến trong câu phủ định hoặc chỉ là thông tin phụ.

### 2. Phân tích chi tiết:

### Question 11

**Phân tích:**
- *"the first thing that attracts them isn't so much the salary... It's the idea of getting a massive **25% off everything**."*
- **25% off** = **staff discount** → ✔ Option B
- Salary được nhắc nhưng bị phủ định (*isn't so much*); working hours không được dẫn dắt là điểm thu hút.

**Đáp án: B**

### Question 12

**Phân tích:**
- *"**Where we are different** is in our approach to making a profit. Other companies think in the short term, whereas we always take a more strategic view, **looking three or five years ahead**."*
- *looking three or five years ahead* = **long-term planning** → ✔ Option A

**Đáp án: A**

### Question 13

**Phân tích:**
- *"they're expected to **put in extra effort** and **take an active role in running the company**."*
- Chủ động điều hành công ty = được giao nhiều trách nhiệm hơn = **more responsibility** → ✔ Option C

**Đáp án: C**

### Question 14

**Phân tích:**
- *"Last year it was **15%**, about two months' salary, which is pretty **standard**."*
- *standard* = **typical**; 8% là mức bất thường (*unusual*), 20% cũng hiếm.

**Đáp án: B**

### Question 15

**Phân tích:**
- *"It's for anyone from cleaners to directors with a **minimum of three months' service**."*
- 6 tháng là để nhận thêm trợ cấp, không phải mức tối thiểu để được dùng holiday centres.

**Đáp án: C**

### Question 16 -- The Grange

**Phân tích:**
- *"the **board of directors always have their annual meeting there**."*
- *board of directors* = **senior management** → thường xuyên sử dụng → Option **C**

**Đáp án: C**

### Question 17 -- Meadow House

**Phân tích:**
- *"unlike other hotels, you have access to the kitchen, so it **feels more like staying with a friend**."*
- *staying with a friend* ≈ *being in someone's home* → Option **F**

**Đáp án: F**

### Question 18 -- Coombe Manor

**Phân tích:**
- *"It's a **paradise for kids**, as there's plenty of space to run wild and also an indoor pool."*
- *paradise for kids* = *suitable for families with children* → Option **B**

**Đáp án: B**

### Question 19 -- The Barn

**Phân tích:**
- *"I recently **celebrated my 25th wedding anniversary**. You can **rent the whole place out for a large group of up to 40 friends**."*
- Tổ chức tiệc kỉ niệm, cho thuê nguyên khu = *available for celebrations and parties* → Option **A**

**Đáp án: A**

### Question 20 -- The Stable

**Phân tích:**
- *"**Ideal for weekends.**"* = *good for short breaks* → Option **G**

**Đáp án: G**

::: cdtranscripttable

| Speaker | Dialogue |
|---------|----------|
| MAN | Hello. |
| WOMAN | Oh, good morning. I'm calling on behalf of my 13-year-old son Jacob. He's interested in learning to sail. |
| MAN | Great. Well, we have what we call a Taster Day on the 22nd of June. |
| WOMAN | I'm not sure he can make it, but we'll see. And Jacob's really interested, though. So can you tell me about the courses? |
| MAN | OK. We have two types of courses for children under 16, two-day and five-day. |
| WOMAN | Could you explain the difference? |
| MAN | Well, the two-day one just covers basic techniques in sailing. And on this course, **the kids always sail with the instructor they've been allocated.** **(Q1)** |
| WOMAN | Well, that's good to know. |
| MAN | Yeah. And **the cost is £65.** **(Q2)** |
| WOMAN | Oh, I thought it was more than that. I heard it was £125. |
| MAN | That's for the five-day course. |
| WOMAN | OK, I presume that course goes into much more detail. |
| MAN | Yes. And from the third day, the kids are given the choice to sail on their own if they want. We use the same types of boats, but we help them learn more techniques, in particular, how to manage the boat in a variety of types of weather. |
| WOMAN | I think Jacob would enjoy that course, but **do you give them a certificate if they complete the course?** |
| MAN | **Absolutely. We give them out on the final day, which a lot of the kids really like.** **(Q3)** |
| WOMAN | Great. Could you tell me a bit more about what the children have to bring and so forth? |
| MAN | Well, the most important thing is the clothing. It goes without saying that whatever the time of year, they should have plenty of warm clothes, as it can be quite cool out on the water. |
| WOMAN | What about footwear? Do they have to wear anything special? |
| MAN | **The only rule is that they've got to have shoes of some kind.** **(Q4)** They mustn't have bare feet. |
| WOMAN | OK. |
| MAN | And **we suggest the kids pack an extra pair of trousers. You know, waterproof ones.** **(Q5)** They don't have to wear them unless it starts to rain. |
| WOMAN | Fine. So basically, it's just using your common sense. |
| MAN | Yes. Oh, and **one other thing we do say is that the children mustn't wear jeans** **(Q6)** because they get extremely heavy when they're wet. |
| WOMAN | Oh, OK. |
| WOMAN | One thing I forgot to ask is whether it's possible to get a meal anywhere. |
| MAN | I'm afraid not. But **we do have a shop called the Marina Stores where he can get a snack.** **(Q7)** |
| WOMAN | OK. And before I forget, when I drop Jacob off, where can I park? |
| MAN | Good question. There are spaces near the entrance, but you have to pay for them. But **there's no charge for the car park just by the bridge.** **(Q8)** Do you know it? |
| WOMAN | Oh yes, I know what you mean. |
| MAN | Now we do need you to fill in a registration form. You should send that in a couple of weeks before the course starts. The form is on the website, but we ask you to print it off and add your signature. |
| WOMAN | Yes, of course. What sort of information do you need? |
| MAN | About your child's swimming ability, any special requirements, and so on. |
| WOMAN | **Will you need to know who his doctor is?** |
| MAN | **Yes.** **(Q9)** |
| WOMAN | We've just recently changed surgery, so I'll make a note to get that. |
| MAN | And one more thing, **there's a separate form on the website, which is optional, that asks whether you're willing for photographs to be taken during the course.** **(Q10)** |
| WOMAN | Yes, of course. No problem. |
| MAN | Great. So I'll send you the details. |

:::

SECTION 2

Good morning. My name's Mark Bergin. I'm a Human Resources Manager at Johnson Jones, and I'm here to tell you about what it's like working for one of the country's best employers. The company has 225 department stores and owns a further 150 supermarkets. At our latest store opening, we had over 9,000 applicants for 750 posts. So why do so many people want to work for us? Well, the first thing that attracts them isn't so much the salary, although that is a little above average. **It's the idea of getting a massive 25% off everything from washing machines to designer handbags.** **(Q11)** People are prepared to put in long hours to get those kinds of perks.

Johnson Jones has been going strong for nearly 150 years and has outlived many of its strongest competitors. Of course, this has something to do with the high-quality products that we sell and our motto, 'better for less,' which means customers know they're getting good value for money. But we're not alone in that. **Where we are different is in our approach to making a profit. Other companies think in the short term, whereas we always take a more strategic view, looking three or five years ahead.** **(Q12)**

The company isn't owned by a family or shareholders. It's a partnership, which means it's owned by its full-time employees, who are all partners and have a share in the company. **In return for the very generous benefits they get, they're expected to put in extra effort and take an active role in running the company.** **(Q13)** This means they're involved in things like making decisions about pension contributions, for example.

Another very attractive reason for working for us is the annual bonus, which we get as a percentage of our salary. **Last year it was 15%, about two months' salary, which is pretty standard.** **(Q14)** It can go down to 8%, but that's unusual, and a couple of times it's even been above 20%. Some people use it to pay off loans, others save it for a deposit on a house.

The most popular benefit for partners are the subsidized holiday centres, which are owned by the company. Once a year, partners can book in for up to two weeks. **It's for anyone from cleaners to directors with a minimum of three months' service.** **(Q15)** After six months' service, partners are entitled to a further subsidy on the cost of their stay.

---

Let me just give you an idea of what's on offer at the holiday centres. **The Grange**, which is the largest of the holiday centres, is used for conferences by the company, and **the board of directors always have their annual meeting there.** **(Q16)** It's all very informal, not like a normal conference centre.

**Meadow House** is located in Kent, which is famous for its apples and other fruit, and it's a large country house. What's unusual about it is that, unlike other hotels, you have access to the kitchen, so **it feels more like staying with a friend.** **(Q17)**

**Coombe Manor** is a working farm. There are loads of cows and sheep, none of which actually end up on our supermarket shelves. **It's a paradise for kids,** **(Q18)** as there's plenty of space to run wild and also an indoor pool. You can choose to stay in cottages or camp by the lake.

**The Barn** is where I recently **celebrated my 25th wedding anniversary. You can rent the whole place out for a large group of up to 40 friends.** **(Q19)** We got all our food delivered from the nearest Johnson Jones supermarket, so there was no cooking to be done. Perfect. All that was missing was the spa, which should be opening next year.

**The Stable** is often hard to get into because it's so popular. That's partly because it's right on the beach with beautiful sea views and only an hour by train from London. **Ideal for weekends.** **(Q20)** The Company Sailing Club is located there, which was started by the Company's founder in 1885.
