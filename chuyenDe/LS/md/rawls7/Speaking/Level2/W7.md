## PART 1

### Gift

#### 1. Have you ever received a great gift?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- I once received a high-quality camera from my older brother.
- It was a total surprise for my twentieth birthday.
- This gift sparked my interest in photography as a serious hobby.

I've definitely received some wonderful gifts, but the most memorable one was a high-quality digital camera from my older brother. This gift was significant because it actually sparked my interest in photography as a serious hobby. Since then, I've taken thousands of photos, and I still treasure the camera because it reminds me of my brother's support for my creative interests.

#### 2. What gift have you received recently?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Recently, a colleague gave me a very nice book about self-improvement.
- I received it as a \"thank you\" for helping with a project.
- It is quite an interesting read and has some very practical advice.

#### 3. Do you think you are good at choosing gifts?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- I'd like to think I'm fairly decent at picking out presents.
- I usually pay close attention to my friends' hobbies throughout the year.
- However, I sometimes struggle when buying for people I don't know well.

#### 4. What do you consider when choosing a gift?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- The most important factor for me is the recipient's personal taste.
- I also try to find something unique that they wouldn\'t buy for themselves.
- Budget is a consideration, but the sentimental value matters much more to me.

### Hobby

#### 1. Do you have any hobbies?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- To be honest, I have a wide range of interests and hobbies.
- Currently, I'm quite passionate about photography and taking street photos.
- It's a great way for me to capture beautiful moments in life.

To be honest, I have a wide range of interests, but currently, I'm quite passionate about photography. Whenever I have some spare time, I enjoy wandering around the city to capture beautiful street scenes and natural landscapes. It's a fantastic way for me to express my creativity and preserve memorable moments in life.

#### 2. Did you have any hobbies when you were a child?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Back in my childhood, I was obsessed with building Lego sets.
- I could spend hours creating different shapes like cars or houses.
- It was a fun hobby that helped me improve my imagination.

#### 3. Do you have a hobby that you\'ve had since childhood?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Actually, playing the piano is a hobby I've maintained for years.
- I started taking lessons when I was seven years old.
- Even though I'm busy now, I still play to relieve stress.

#### 4. Do you have the same hobbies as your family members?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Not really, as everyone in my family has their own preferences.
- My mother enjoys cooking, while my father is a huge fan of football.
- We only share a common interest in watching action movies on weekends.

## PART 2

::: prompt
**Describe an occasion when you were not allowed to use your mobile phone**
- When it was
- Where it was
- Why you were not allowed to use your mobile phone
- And how you felt about it
:::

**Sample answer:**

I'm going to talk about an occasion when I was prohibited from using my smartphone. This occurred last summer during my cousin's traditional wedding ceremony, which was held in a very peaceful temple. The atmosphere was quite sacred, and everyone was dressed in formal attire to show their respect.

The reason for this restriction was to maintain the solemnity of the event. The priest requested that all guests switch off their devices or keep them on silent because the ringing of a phone would be considered extremely disrespectful and disruptive to the prayers. Consequently, I had to keep my phone tucked away in my pocket for the entire duration of the ceremony, which lasted nearly three hours.

Initially, I felt a bit anxious and disconnected, as I have a habit of constantly checking my notifications. However, as the ceremony progressed, I began to appreciate the silence. Without the distraction of social media, I was able to fully immerse myself in the beautiful rituals and connect with my family members. Looking back, I felt that being \"offline\" for a while was quite refreshing and allowed me to be truly present during such a significant family milestone.

## PART 3

#### 1. Do you think it is necessary to have laws on the use of mobile phones?

In my opinion, implementing laws regarding mobile phone usage is absolutely essential, particularly for public safety. The most obvious reason is to prevent distracted driving, which is a leading cause of road accidents worldwide. Furthermore, regulations are needed in sensitive areas like exam halls or courtrooms to maintain integrity and privacy. Without these legal boundaries, people might ignore social etiquette and put others at risk. Consequently, government regulations play a vital role in ensuring that technology is used responsibly and safely.

#### 2. Is it a waste of time to take pictures with mobile phones?

I wouldn\'t say it's a waste of time, as photography allows individuals to document significant life events and personal milestones. It serves as a creative outlet for many people who enjoy capturing beautiful scenery or candid moments. However, it becomes problematic when people spend more time behind the lens than actually enjoying the experience. If someone is constantly obsessed with getting the perfect shot for social media, they might miss out on the actual joy of the moment, which is unfortunate.

#### 3. What positive and negative impact do mobile phones have on friendship?

Mobile phones have revolutionized the way we maintain friendships by allowing constant communication regardless of geographical distance. This is a huge advantage for staying connected with long-distance friends. On the other hand, the \"phubbing\" phenomenon where people ignore their companions in favor of their phones can seriously damage real-life intimacy. It often leads to a lack of deep, meaningful conversation during face-to-face interactions. Therefore, while phones bridge the distance, they can also create a digital barrier between friends sitting right next to each other.
