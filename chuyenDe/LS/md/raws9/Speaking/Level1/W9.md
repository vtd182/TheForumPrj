## PART 1

### Housework and cooking

#### 1. Did you do some house cleaning when you were young?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Yes, I helped my parents with the house when I was a child.
- I usually cleaned my own bedroom every weekend to keep it tidy.
- I did not like it very much because I thought it was quite boring.

To be honest, yes, I helped my parents with the house when I was a child. For example, I usually cleaned my own bedroom every weekend to keep it tidy and nice. However, I did not like it very much because I thought it was quite boring and I preferred to play games.

#### 2. What housework do you like or dislike doing?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- I really like washing the dishes after we eat dinner.
- I hate cleaning the floor because it is very hard work.
- I think doing laundry is okay because it is easy.

#### 3. Do you like cooking?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- Yes, I like cooking for my family and friends.
- <!-- -->
- I can cook some simple food like fried rice or soup.
- I feel very happy when people say my food is delicious.

#### 4. How often do you cook?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- I cook almost every day for my lunch and dinner.
- Sometimes I am busy, so I buy food outside.
- I usually cook more often at the weekend.
- Spare time

#### 1. Do you often have free time?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- No, I am very busy with my studies and work.
- I only have a little free time in the evening.
- I use that time to relax and watch TV.

To be honest, no, I do not often have free time because I am very busy with my studies and my work. I usually only have a little bit of free time in the evening after I finish everything. I usually use that time to relax on the sofa and watch some TV programs.

#### 2. What do you usually do in your spare time?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- I like listening to music or playing games on my phone.
- Sometimes I go out to have coffee with my friends.
- I also like sleeping because I am often tired.

#### 3. Do you have more free time on weekdays or at weekends?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- I have more free time at the weekends.
- From Monday to Friday, I have to go to school and work.
- Saturday and Sunday are better for relaxing.

#### 4. Do you have the same hobbies as your family members?

**[Main ideas]{.cdblue}:**

- I have more free time on Sunday.
- On Saturday, I still have some chores to do at home.
- Sunday is the day I can stay in bed longer.

## PART 2

::: prompt
**Describe a bicycle/motorcycle/car trip you would like to go**
- Who you would like to go with
- Where you would like to go
- When you would like to go
- And explain why you would like to go by bicycle/motorcycle/car
:::

I would like to talk about a motorcycle trip that I want to go on in the future. I plan to go with my best friend from high school because we both love adventures and we have a lot of things to talk about. We would like to travel to Da Lat, which is a very famous mountain city in Vietnam. I think the best time to go is during the next summer holiday because the weather will be very nice and sunny.

I want to travel by motorcycle instead of a bus or car for several reasons. First, a motorcycle is very convenient because we can stop anywhere we want to take beautiful photos of the mountains and flowers. Second, I really like the feeling of the wind on my face and smelling the fresh air in the countryside. It is much more exciting than sitting inside a car. Finally, riding a bike is quite cheap, so we can save money for delicious food and a nice hotel. This trip would be a great way for me to relax after a long time of studying and working hard.

## PART 3

#### 1. How are the transportation systems in urban areas and rural areas different?

In my opinion, there are many differences. In urban areas, the transportation system is very modern and fast. There are many buses, taxis, and sometimes trains, so people can move easily. However, in rural areas, there are fewer options. People usually use their own motorbikes or bicycles to travel because there is no public transport. The roads in the countryside are also smaller and sometimes not very good. Therefore, traveling in the city is much more convenient than in the country.

#### 2. Do you think people need to change the way of transportation drastically to protect the environment?

Yes, I think it is very important to change. Now, too many people use private cars and motorbikes, which produce a lot of smoke. This is bad for the air and the planet. If we want to protect the environment, we should use public transport like buses or electric trains more often. Also, walking or cycling for short trips is a great idea. If everyone changes their habits, the world will become much cleaner and healthier for everyone.

#### 3. Do you think air pollution comes mostly from mobile vehicles?

I agree that mobile vehicles are a big reason for air pollution. In big cities, there are millions of cars and motorbikes on the road every day. They release a lot of dangerous gas into the air, which makes it hard to breathe. However, I also think that big factories and construction sites contribute to this problem. So, while vehicles are a main cause, they are not the only thing that makes the air dirty in our world.
